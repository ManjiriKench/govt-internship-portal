Our Vision: Empowering the Future of Bharat

The Ministry of Corporate Affairs (MoCA) is committed to fostering a new generation of skilled professionals who are ready to contribute to nation-building. This AI-powered portal is our commitment to the youth of Bharat. By leveraging intelligent matching algorithms, we aim to bridge the gap between academic learning and practical application in government sectors. Our mission is to democratize access to high-quality internships, ensuring that every motivated student, regardless of their background, has the opportunity to gain invaluable experience, develop a civic sense, and become a part of India's growth story.

How the Internship Scheme Works

1

Register

Students sign up on the MCA Internship Portal with basic details and academic credentials.

2

AI Matching

The system uses AI to match students with internships relevant to their skills and interests.

3

Apply & Work

Students can apply, attend selection rounds, and work with top government bodies and organizations.

4

Certification

Successful completion of internships earns official certificates from MCA.

Are you Eligible?

Indian Citizen

Applicants must be a citizen of India.

Academic Enrollment

Currently enrolled in a UG/PG program at a recognized institution.

Strong Academics

A strong academic background is preferred to showcase dedication.

Public Service Motivation

A genuine motivation to contribute to public service projects.

Core Benefits for MCA Internship Scheme

Hands-on Experience

Gain practical experience in government and policy-making.

Real-world Exposure

Exposure to real-world challenges and solutions in public service.

Official Certification

Successful completion earns an official certificate from MCA.

Professional Networking

Connect with top government officials and institutions.
